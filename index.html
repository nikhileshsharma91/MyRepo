The Festival began last year with 3 concerts, 10 acts in two venues. This year we are trying to bump it up a notch – 5 concerts over two weekends in October, at a location in Bankstown and a second venue in Campbelltown. We have signed up 18 acts, including 6 from overseas.
We plan on two day shows on the 18th and 19th of October at the Dixon Concert Hall in Bankstown, with the 8th being focused on jazz and the 9th on blues.
The focus of the Festival then moves to Campbelltown at the Tsingos Performing Centre for evening concerts on the 23rd, 24th and 25th of October. The 23rd will be their International Jazz Showcase. The 24th will feature local jazz and blues acts from NSW and the ACT. Headliner and Blues legend A. Sattar and his band “The Data Cablers” will close out a blues spectacular on the 25th.
